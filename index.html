What is a Green New Deal?

The Green New Deal is a congressional resolution to transform every sector of the United State’s political economy and orient it towards 100% clean and renewable energy and guaranteeing a just transition for workers & disenfranchised communities - all while creating millions of jobs in the process. This Resolution will have a profound impact on policy, institutions, landscapes, and infrastructures throughout the nation.
